Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a registered farmer with DealCraft?

Contact our farmer relations team at farmers@dealcraftpartners.com or visit any of our regional offices with your identification and farm details.

What commodities do you currently trade?

We currently trade in maize, rice, sorghum, soybeans, cocoa, and cashew nuts. We're expanding to more commodities soon.

How does the SecureStore system work?

SecureStore is our electronic warehouse receipt system that digitizes stored commodities, allowing them to be used as collateral for loans or traded on our platform.

Do you offer training for farmers?

Yes, we regularly conduct training sessions on best farming practices, post-harvest handling, and financial literacy. Check our events calendar for upcoming sessions.
