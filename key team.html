Our Leadership Team

Meet the visionaries driving agricultural innovation in Nigeria

Executive Leadership

Oluwaseun Adebayo

Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in agricultural finance and commodity trading across West Africa, Oluwaseun founded DealCraft with a vision to transform Nigeria's agricultural sector. His expertise in financial innovation has been instrumental in developing our electronic warehouse receipt system.

Amina Ibrahim

Chief Operations Officer

Amina is a specialist in supply chain management with extensive expertise in agricultural logistics and warehouse operations. She oversees our nationwide network of storage facilities and ensures operational excellence across all our services. Her background includes senior roles at major agribusiness firms.

Chukwudi Okonkwo

Chief Technology Officer

Chukwudi leads our technology initiatives, bringing a wealth of experience in fintech and agricultural technology solutions. He architected our SecureStore electronic warehouse receipt system and continues to drive innovation in our digital platforms. His background includes leadership roles at prominent Nigerian tech companies.
